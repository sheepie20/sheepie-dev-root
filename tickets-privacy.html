Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2025

Simple Tickets is a Discord bot designed to provide ticketing and support functionality within Discord servers.

Information We Collect

  • Server IDs – to store ticket system configurations.
  • Channel and Message IDs – to manage and create tickets.
  • User IDs – to identify who opens and interacts with tickets.

How We Use Information

We use collected data solely to provide the functionality of the bot. None of your information is sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

All data is stored securely and is only accessible to the bot’s developer.

Data Removal

If you would like your server’s data removed, please contact us via email with your server ID.
